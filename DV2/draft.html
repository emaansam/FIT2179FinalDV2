Performance

The dumbell plot shows the different in percentage of 40+ study scores from 2024 to 2025. Some schools had significant increase, with Bacchus Marsh Grammar having a 19.4% increase in just one year.
St Kevin's College and Loreto Mandevile Hall were the only Catholic schools in Victoria's Top 25 highest performing schools. Majority of the Top 25 High Performing schools were independent schools.
The diverging bar chart highlights how each school ranking changed between 2024 and 2025. Many schools increased in ranking, including Bacchus Marsh Grammar, increasing significantly by 63 ranks, becoming rank 3 in 2025. 3 Schools did not change in ranking, including Ballarat Clarendon College who has kept its Rank 1 title untouched. Some schools decreaesd in rank, including Mount Scopus Memorial College.
